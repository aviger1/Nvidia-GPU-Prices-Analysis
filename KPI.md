&#x20;   Does a higher street-price premium (scalping) actually correlate with lower customer satisfaction?

&#x09;*Findings*: Analysing the Data revealed a small **positive** correlation between street price and user satisfaction. It would seem as the street price

&#x09;rises, reflecting a greater demand in the market, a transaction in the "official" channels reflects positively on the user satisfaction from the 	sale.

&#x09;*Action*: It is recommended for the company to stock up on in-demand models, allowing the price to rise through lowering the supply in the market, and reap both the resulting profit and the increased user satisfaction.



&#x20;   Compare revenue concentration: does Data Center AI (fewer, bigger sales) or Consumer Gaming (many small sales) drive more total revenue?

&#x09;*Findings*: **total revenue is most affected by the sales to Data Centers and related customers.** It drives app. 60% of the company's revenue. Of that revenue, 54% is driven by hyperscale AI data centers, 26% to AI research, and 19% to crypto mining. Of the 40% not relating to Data Centers, gaming dominates the revenue share with 80%, the rest to content creation.

&#x09;*Action*: It is recommended for the company to continue focusing on the Data center market, especially regarding the 'Hyperscale AI Data Center 'customer market, as it drives the most revenue.

&#x20;   Build a time series of average street-price premium over 2024-2026 — does it spike around specific stock-status periods?

&#x09;*Findings:* Current premiums relating to stock status are: 'Sold-out' \~ 40%, 'Backordered \~ 31%', 'Low Stock \~ 14%', 'In Stock \~ 5%'. This trend has subsided from 2024, probably relating to the emerging of the hyperscale AI Data Center Market, which currently the market has better adapted to its effects on demand. Also, the models 'A100','H100' and L40S are most affected by stock shortages, maybe because of their rarity.

&#x09;*Action*: The company should try and keep the stock status as out of stock or backordered in order to increase the prices and increase profit.
